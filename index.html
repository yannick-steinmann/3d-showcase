Product Manager since 2017

I have been working in startups and tech since 2017. My last stations were as a Product Lead at Friendsurance and as a freelance Product consultant/coach for a variety of startup clients.

Focus on WHAT to build

Product Discovery is crucial for a product's success but in my experience it falls short far too often. That's why I put an extra effort into Discovery. At Friendsurance, I have set up a Product Discovery process for a team of 20+ contributors, which enabled us to get budget and focus for the necessary research, validate ideas and fill the product roadmap with features that actually made a difference.

Digital products allow us to deeply understand user behavior and preferences by leveraging data. I combine this data with my knowledge of growth models, experiment results and qualitative research to create a digital experience that acquires, activates and retains users sustainably.
